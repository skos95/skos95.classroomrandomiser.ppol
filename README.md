Privacy Policy for Classroom Randomiser

Last updated: 2/2/2026

No Data Collection

Classroom Randomiser does not collect, store, or share any personal information or user data. All class lists and seating arrangements you create remain solely on your device and are never transmitted to our servers or any third parties.
What This Means:


We don't collect names, email addresses, or any identifying information

Student names you enter into the app stay on your device only

We don't track how you use the app

We don't use cookies, analytics, or advertising trackers

We have no access to your data

Your Control

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have complete control. If you uninstall the app, all data is removed from your device.

Children's Privacy

While this app is designed for teachers and may involve entering student names, we do not collect any information about children or anyone else.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever change our data practices, we will update this privacy policy and notify users through the app.
